How it works

Everything you need
to feel supported

🎙️
Talk or type
Tap the mic and just speak. Your words are transcribed and sent automatically. The AI responds both in text and voice — so it feels like a real conversation.
🧠
Trained for empathy
Our AI is grounded in evidence-based mental health and addiction recovery frameworks — not generic chatbot responses. It listens. It reflects. It never dismisses.
🔒
Private by design
Your conversations are yours alone. We use secure, encrypted sessions. We don't sell data. No ads. No profiling. Just a safe space to open up.
🌍
Speaks your language
Available in English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, and Yoruba — with voice support in all five. Support shouldn't have a language barrier.
